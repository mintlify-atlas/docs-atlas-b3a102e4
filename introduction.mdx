## Core Systems

The vault is built on 3 core engines that work together:

### 1. Vibe Coding Blueprint Builder
Turn any app idea into a complete build plan with features, user flow, design direction, and starter prompts for all three complexity levels (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced).

### 2. Brand Your App
Niche-specific color palettes, typography, and design direction for 9 niches — so your app looks professional without hiring a designer.

### 3. Ship It
Deploy your app to the internet in 6 steps with our proven deployment workflow.

  **Start here if you're new:** Pick a Beginner blueprint from the library, use the starter prompt in Google AI Studio (free), and deploy to GitHub Pages. You can have a live app this week.
</Tip>
