## Stop Learning. Start Building.

The **100-Day App Challenge** is a personal transformation tool that generates app ideas, tracks progress, and transforms you from learner to builder through shipping 1 app per day.

<Note>
  **The core philosophy:** The gap between knowing and having is called EXECUTION. This challenge builds your execution muscle.
</Note>

## The Philosophy

### The Problem

- Traditional coding has a 2-4 year learning curve
- YouTube teaches you everything... and you build nothing
- You have 47 browser tabs open and $0 in results
- Knowledge without output is just entertainment

### The Solution

**Ship daily.** Not perfect apps. Not production-grade systems. Just working, deployed apps that solve real problems.

<Tip>
  **The 100-Day Challenge is not about perfection.** It's about building the muscle of completion. The instinct to ship. The habit of execution.
</Tip>

### What You Get

After 100 days:

- ✅ **100 live apps** with shareable URLs
- ✅ **A massive portfolio** of real projects
- ✅ **The execution habit** — you complete things now
- ✅ **Technical versatility** across different app types
- ✅ **Speed and confidence** — you can ship fast
- ✅ **Proof of work** for clients, employers, or yourself

<Warning>
  Most people quit between Day 7 and Day 15. The challenge isn't building apps — it's shipping consistently when motivation fades. That's where transformation happens.
</Warning>

## The Methodology

### Daily Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Morning: Generate Idea (5 min)">
    Open the app, select complexity level, hit "GENERATE TODAY'S APP"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Planning: Pick Blueprint or Build Custom (10 min)">
    Use Blueprint Builder if it matches a pattern, or create custom prompt
  </Step>
  <Step title="Building: Follow 7-Step Workflow (1-8 hours)">
    DESCRIBE → SCAFFOLD → VALIDATE → SECURE → ITERATE → POLISH → DOCUMENT
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy: Ship It (15 min)">
    GitHub Pages for Quick, Vercel for Medium/Complex
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track: Mark Complete in App (2 min)">
    Update progress, maintain streak, celebrate the win
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Recommended Complexity Mix

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Week 1-2">
    **100% Quick complexity**
    
    Focus on speed and consistency. Ship every day. Build the habit.
    
    Goal: Prove you can ship 14 days straight.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Week 3-6">
    **70% Quick, 30% Medium**
    
    Start adding variety. Most days are Quick, but challenge yourself 2-3 times per week.
    
    Goal: Maintain streak while leveling up.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Week 7-12">
    **50% Quick, 40% Medium, 10% Complex**
    
    Balanced approach. Save Complex for weekends or days with more time.
    
    Goal: Build versatility across all complexity levels.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Week 13-14">
    **Your choice**
    
    By now you know your rhythm. Pick based on available time and energy.
    
    Goal: Finish strong. Ship Day 100.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
