## About This Niche

Beauty and skincare apps help users build personalized routines, track product inventories, match foundation shades, and manage beauty businesses. This niche values elegance, precision, and self-care.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Visual Language" icon="palette">
    Soft pinks and mauves that feel luxurious and refined without being overly feminine. Think high-end beauty counter.
  </Card>
  <Card title="User Mindset" icon="brain">
    Detail-oriented. Want to track products, ingredients, routines, and results with precision.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## 4 Beauty & Skincare Blueprints

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="BS-01: Skincare Routine Builder" icon="droplet" color="#BC6C8A">
    **Level:** Beginner • **Time:** 2-4 hours
    
    Personalized AM/PM skincare routine builder. Users select skin type, concerns, and products to create a custom routine.
    
    **Core Features:**
    - Skin type quiz
    - Concern selector (acne, aging, dryness, etc.)
    - AM/PM routine builder
    - Product order recommendations
    - Routine reminders
    - Save favorite routines
    
    **Monetization:** $19-$47 for individuals, $297 for estheticians
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="BS-02: The Shade Matcher" icon="palette" color="#DAA0B6">
    **Level:** Intermediate • **Time:** 6-10 hours
    
    Foundation shade matching tool across brands. Find your shade in any brand based on one known match.
    
    **Core Features:**
    - Brand and shade database
    - "Find my shade" wizard
    - Cross-brand shade matching
    - Undertone analysis
    - Save shade matches
    - Community shade reviews
    
    **Monetization:** $29-$97 beauty tool, $4.99/mo premium database
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="BS-03: The Product Tracker" icon="box" color="#F2D0D9">
    **Level:** Intermediate • **Time:** 6-10 hours
    
    Beauty product inventory manager with expiration alerts and ingredient tracking.
    
    **Core Features:**
    - Add products with photos
    - Track purchase date and expiration
    - Expiration alerts
    - Ingredient list per product
    - Category organization (skincare, makeup, haircare)
    - Export inventory list
    
    **Monetization:** $19-$67 for beauty enthusiasts, $197 for MUAs
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="BS-04: Beauty Brand Launcher" icon="store" color="#F2D0D9">
    **Level:** Advanced • **Time:** 15-25 hours
    
    Mini business toolkit for launching a beauty brand. Product catalog, ingredient sourcing, cost calculator, and vendor management.
    
    **Core Features:**
    - Product formulation tracker
    - Ingredient sourcing database
    - Cost per unit calculator
    - Vendor contact management
    - Batch production tracker
    - Compliance checklist
    - Launch timeline planner
    
    **Monetization:** $497-$1,997 for beauty entrepreneurs, $49/mo business subscription
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Design Direction

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Color Palette">
    | Role | Color Name | Hex | Usage |
    |------|------------|-----|-------|
    | **Primary** | Rose Quartz | `#BC6C8A` | Main buttons, headers, navigation |
    | **Secondary** | Mauve Silk | `#DAA0B6` | Supporting elements, hover states |
    | **Accent** | Petal Blush | `#F2D0D9` | Highlights, success states, badges |
    | **Background** | Powder White | `#FFF5F8` | Page background, card surfaces |
    | **Text** | Deep Plum | `#5C2D42` | Body text, headings, labels |
    
    <Tip>
    Use gradient overlays from rose quartz to mauve silk for premium features and hero sections
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Typography">
    ### Heading Font: Playfair Display
    - **Weights:** 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)
    - **Use for:** Page titles, section headers, product names
    - **Why:** Elegant serif that feels luxurious and high-end
    
    ### Body Font: Lato
    - **Weights:** 400 (Regular), 700 (Bold)
    - **Use for:** Body text, product descriptions, UI labels
    - **Why:** Clean, highly readable, pairs beautifully with Playfair
    
    ```html
    <link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Playfair+Display:wght@600;700&family=Lato:wght@400;700&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
    ```
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Design Rules">
    ### Do's
    <Check>Use soft shadows and subtle gradients — Creates depth without harshness</Check>
    <Check>Display product photos prominently — Beauty is visual</Check>
    <Check>Use generous white space — Feels premium and uncluttered</Check>
    <Check>Add subtle animations on interactions — Smooth, elegant transitions</Check>
    
    ### Don'ts
    <Warning>Don't use harsh blacks — Stick to the deep plum text color</Warning>
    <Warning>Don't use bright, neon accents — Keep colors soft and sophisticated</Warning>
    <Warning>Don't clutter with too many CTAs — One clear action per section</Warning>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Icon Style">
    **Recommendation:** Outlined icons with elegant stroke
    
    Delicate outlined icons match the refined, elegant feel of the niche.
    
    **Icon Sources:**
    - [Lucide Icons](https://lucide.dev)
    - [Heroicons Outline](https://heroicons.com)
    - [Feather Icons](https://feathericons.com)
    
    **Priority Icons:** Sparkles, Droplet, Sun, Moon, Heart, Star, ShoppingBag, Camera, Calendar
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Consumer vs Alchemist

| The Consumer... | The Alchemist... |
|-----------------|------------------|
| Buys skincare products, forgets what order to apply them, routine feels random | Builds Skincare Routine Builder with AM/PM order and reminders |
| Knows their foundation shade in one brand, guesses when buying from new brands | Creates Shade Matcher with cross-brand database, finds perfect match every time |
| Has 47 beauty products, no idea when they expire, uses old product and breaks out | Builds Product Tracker with expiration alerts, knows exactly what's still good |

## Learning Path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with BS-01 (Beginner)">
    Build Skincare Routine Builder to learn: quiz logic, routine organization, localStorage, reminder system
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Level up to BS-02 or BS-03 (Intermediate)">
    Build Shade Matcher (database search, cross-referencing) OR Product Tracker (inventory management, date calculations, alerts)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Go full-stack with BS-04 (Advanced)">
    Build Beauty Brand Launcher to master: complex data relationships, cost calculations, vendor management, multi-step workflows
  </Step>
</Steps>
