## The Builder's Journey

Every community space connects to your progression as a builder:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Learn">
    Start with Flash Modules 01-10 to master the fundamentals. Ask questions in **Flash Module Q&A** as you work through each lesson.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose">
    Browse the Blueprint Index and pick an app to build. Discuss adaptations and share ideas in **Blueprint Lab**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Build">
    Attend Vibe Coding Wednesday live classes and build alongside the community. Get unstuck in **Vibe Coding Help** when you hit roadblocks.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ship">
    Deploy your app during Ship It Friday classes. Post your live URL in **Ship It Showcase** — every deployment is a win worth celebrating.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compete">
    Join the weekly Blueprint Challenge announced every Monday. Submit your build and compete for App of the Week recognition.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Win">
    Standout apps from Ship It Showcase get nominated for **App of the Week** every Friday, with winners announced Sunday.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Collaborate">
    Find your people in **Build Partners**. Accountability partners, co-building sessions, and niche study groups all start here.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
No dead ends in this flow. Every stage has a community space that supports it, and every space feeds back into the next stage of building.
</Note>

## Live Classes

The community rhythm follows a weekly live class schedule:

| Day | Class | Duration | What Happens |
|-----|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Monday** | Blueprint Monday | 60 min | Spotlight a niche, walk through a blueprint, live Builder demo |
| **Wednesday** | Vibe Coding Wednesday | 90 min | Recap blueprint, flash concept, live build session |
| **Friday** | Ship It Friday | 60 min | Deploy the week's build, member showcase, App of the Week |

<Tip>
Attend live when you can, but recordings are always available. The magic happens when you build alongside the community.
</Tip>

### How to Get the Most Value

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Share Consistently" icon="paper-plane">
    The builders who grow fastest aren't the ones who build the most — they're the ones who share consistently. Ship it, share it, repeat.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Be Specific" icon="magnifying-glass">
    "It was hard" teaches nothing. "The timer stopped in background tabs because setInterval pauses" teaches everyone who reads it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Respond to Others" icon="comments">
    Reply to at least ONE other person's post when you share your work. Community is built through conversation, not broadcast.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ship Ugly, Iterate Publicly" icon="rocket">
    Don't wait for perfection. If it's deployed and working, it's ready to share. v1 teaches you what v2 should be.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Recognition & Rewards

### App of the Week

Every week, one standout app from the community gets featured:

- **Friday**: Nominations open after Ship It Friday class
- **Saturday**: Community voting on nominated apps
- **Sunday**: Winner announced and pinned for the week

Winners are featured in the weekly newsletter and may be showcased in future live classes.

### Challenge Completions

Every Blueprint Challenge submission that meets the requirements gets:

- Public recognition in the challenge thread
- Personal feedback from DESI
- Entry into App of the Week consideration
- Addition to the community showcase archive

<Note>
Your live URL is proof of work — more valuable than any certificate you could earn. Every app you ship and share builds your portfolio and credibility as a builder.
</Note>

## Getting Help

When you get stuck, the community has your back:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Flash Module 10">
    Flash Module 10: Debug Like a Builder covers common issues and debugging strategies.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Post in Vibe Coding Help">
    Use the posting template: what you're building, what you expected, what happened, error messages, and what you've tried.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Include Context">
    Screenshots, error messages, and specific descriptions help others help you faster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The fastest way to learn is to help someone else. Answer questions in Vibe Coding Help even if you're not an expert — teaching solidifies your own understanding.
</Tip>
